<p><strong>The Bariatric Surgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Assisting Devices</li><li>Implantable Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bariatric surgical devices market comprises two primary types: assisting devices and implantable devices. Assisting devices, such as gastric balloons and endoscopic staplers, aid in weight loss procedures by promoting early satiety and limiting food intake. Implantable devices, including adjustable gastric bands and gastric bypass systems, are surgically placed to alter the digestive system, reducing stomach capacity and nutrient absorption. Together, these devices enhance weight management efforts for individuals struggling with obesity, improving health outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
