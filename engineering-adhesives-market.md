<p><strong>The Engineering Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive acrylic adhesive (SGA or UV adhesive)</li><li>Anaerobic Adhesives</li><li>Cyanoacrylate adhesive</li><li>Epoxy adhesive</li><li>Silicone adhesive</li><li>Polyurethane Adhesives (solvent-free reactive PU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineering adhesives market consists of various types, each with unique properties. Reactive acrylic adhesives, including SGA and UV adhesives, offer strong bonding with quick curing. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, ideal for metal bonding. Cyanoacrylate adhesives, or super glues, provide rapid adhesion. Epoxy adhesives are known for their excellent mechanical strength and chemical resistance. Silicone adhesives are flexible and withstand extreme temperatures, while solvent-free polyurethane adhesives offer durability and environmental safety, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
