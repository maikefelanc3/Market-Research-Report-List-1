<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering System Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems use electric motors to assist vehicle steering. The four main types include:</p><p>1. **CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering)**: Integrated into the steering column, offering direct assistance.</p><p>2. **REPS (Rack Electric Power Steering)**: Positioned at the steering rack, providing greater efficiency and feedback.</p><p>3. **PEPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering)**: Located at the pinion gear, known for its compact design.</p><p>4. **EHPS (Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering)**: Combines electric and hydraulic systems for enhanced control and responsiveness. Each type caters to various vehicle designs and driver preferences.</p></p>
