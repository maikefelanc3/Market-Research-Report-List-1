<p><p>Diffusion vacuum pumps and vapor jet pumps are essential components in the vacuum technology market. Oil diffusion pumps utilize heated oil to create a high vacuum by directing gas molecules towards the pump's exhaust. They are known for their efficiency and ability to achieve low pressures. Vapor jet pumps, on the other hand, utilize high-velocity jets of vapor to entrain gases, offering an effective means of creating a vacuum in industrial applications. Both types cater to different operational needs and industries.</p></p>
