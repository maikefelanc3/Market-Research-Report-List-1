<p><p>A Continuous Passive Motion (CPM) device is a therapeutic tool designed to aid recovery by gently mobilizing joints after surgery or injury. It helps reduce pain and swelling, improve circulation, and facilitate the healing process by keeping the affected joint in motion without active effort from the patient. Commonly used in rehabilitation settings post-knee, shoulder, and hip surgeries, these devices are recognized for their effectiveness in restoring range of motion.</p><p>The Continuous Passive Motion Device Market is witnessing notable growth, driven by an increasing aging population and a corresponding rise in orthopedic surgeries and rehabilitation procedures. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period. The growing awareness of orthopedic rehabilitation benefits and the rising prevalence of chronic joint disorders further fuel market growth. Furthermore, the trend towards outpatient surgeries and home healthcare solutions is also propelling the adoption of CPM devices, as they become more accessible and essential for effective recovery processes.</p></p>
