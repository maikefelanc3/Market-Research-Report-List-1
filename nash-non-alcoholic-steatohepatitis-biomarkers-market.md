<p><p>The NASH biomarkers market includes various types that aid in diagnosing and monitoring non-alcoholic steatohepatitis. Serum biomarkers assess liver function and fat accumulation, while hepatic fibrosis biomarkers evaluate liver scarring severity. Apoptosis biomarkers indicate programmed cell death related to liver damage, and oxidative stress biomarkers measure cell damage caused by free radicals. Other biomarkers may encompass genetic indicators or novel assays. Together, these biomarkers facilitate early detection, treatment efficacy, and disease progression monitoring in NASH patients.</p></p>
