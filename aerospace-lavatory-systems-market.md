<p><p>Aerospace lavatory systems are categorized primarily into four types. Reusable lavatory systems allow for the collection and reuse of waste, reducing environmental impact. Recirculating lavatory systems process waste in a closed loop, utilizing chemicals to minimize odor and maintain hygiene. Vacuum toilet lavatory systems use suction to efficiently remove waste, minimizing water usage and weight, essential for aircraft performance. Other market types may include hybrid systems or innovative designs that combine features of the above systems for enhanced functionality.</p></p>
