<p><p>Ultrafilter market types, specifically Tube Type and Roll Type, distinguish between two primary configurations used in filtration processes. The Tube Type features cylindrical membranes that optimize fluid flow and separation efficiency, suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, the Roll Type employs flat membrane sheets rolled for compactness, maximizing surface area in limited spaces. Both designs aim to enhance filtration performance, enabling industries to achieve higher purity levels in water treatment, food processing, and other critical sectors while considering space and efficiency.</p></p>
