<p><p>The Wireless Handheld Spectrometer market encompasses various types, including FTRI (Fourier Transform Infrared), NIR (Near-Infrared), and Raman spectrometers, along with other specialized devices. FTRI is used for characterizing molecular structures and compositions through infrared absorption. NIR spectrometers analyze the reflectance and absorbance of wavelengths to assess material properties, particularly in agriculture and food quality. Raman spectrometers utilize inelastic scattering of light to identify chemical compounds and materials. Each type serves unique applications across industries such as pharmaceuticals, food safety, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
