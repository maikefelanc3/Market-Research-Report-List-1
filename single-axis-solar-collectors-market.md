Medium-temperature collectors, operating between 80°C and 250°C, are used for processes like space heating and industrial applications. High-temperature collectors exceed 250°C, suitable for electricity generation and concentrated solar power systems. Each type targets different market needs, optimizing energy capture and usage across various sectors.</p></p>
