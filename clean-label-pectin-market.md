<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clean Label Pectin market is primarily divided into two types: High Methoxyl Pectin (HMP) and Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMP). HMP is derived from fruits and requires a higher sugar concentration and acid for gel formation, making it ideal for jams and jellies. In contrast, LMP functions with low sugar levels and is often used in products like dairy and bakery items, enabling healthier formulations. Both types cater to consumer demand for transparent, natural ingredients in food products.</p></p>
