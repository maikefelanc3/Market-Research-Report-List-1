<p><p>The Static Cone Penetrometer market is categorized into two main types: Hand Operated Static Cone Penetrometer and Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometer. Hand Operated models require manual force for penetration, making them cost-effective and easy to use for small projects. In contrast, Self-Start models feature automatic or powered mechanisms that allow for quicker and more efficient soil testing, ideal for larger or more complex sites. Both types are essential for assessing ground conditions in geotechnical engineering and construction.</p></p>
