<p><strong>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs</li><li>Powered Device Controllers & ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) solutions market is categorized into two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and integrated circuits (ICs) and Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs. PSE controllers manage the power delivery from the network switch or injector to the connected devices, ensuring efficient power distribution. In contrast, PD controllers and ICs are embedded in the powered devices, such as IP cameras and phones, enabling them to safely receive and manage the power transmitted over Ethernet cables.</p></p>
