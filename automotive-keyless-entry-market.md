<p><strong>The Automotive Keyless Entry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) System</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive keyless entry market consists of two primary types: Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) and Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) systems. PKE allows users to unlock their vehicle by merely approaching it, using sensors to detect the key fob nearby, enabling keyless access and ignition. In contrast, RKE requires users to press a button on a key fob to unlock and start the vehicle from a distance, offering convenience but requiring manual operation. Both systems enhance security and ease of use in modern vehicles.</p></p>
