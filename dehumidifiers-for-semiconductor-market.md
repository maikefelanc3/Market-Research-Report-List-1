<p><p>The semiconductor market utilizes two main types of dehumidifiers: desiccant and refrigerant types. Desiccant dehumidifiers use hygroscopic materials to absorb moisture from the air, making them ideal for low-humidity environments, ensuring consistent air quality. Refrigerant dehumidifiers, on the other hand, cool air to condense moisture, effectively maintaining humidity levels in larger spaces. Both types are essential for semiconductor manufacturing, where precise humidity control is crucial to avoid defects and ensure product reliability.</p></p>
