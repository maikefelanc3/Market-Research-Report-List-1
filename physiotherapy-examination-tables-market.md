<p><p>The physiotherapy examination tables market can be categorized into three main types: electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric tables feature motorized adjustments for height and positioning, offering convenience and precision for therapists. Manual tables require physical effort to modify their settings, providing a cost-effective option but potentially less ease of use. Hydraulic tables use a pump mechanism to adjust height and angle, combining ease of adjustment with stability. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in clinical settings.</p></p>
