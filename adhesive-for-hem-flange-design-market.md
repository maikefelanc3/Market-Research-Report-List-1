<p><p>The adhesive for hem flange design market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, providing ease of application and faster curing times. They are ideal for automated processes. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application, offering superior bond strength and durability. This type is typically chosen for demanding applications where performance is critical.</p></p>
