<p><p>The Medical Grade FEP market can be divided into two primary types: Extrusion Grade and Injection Grade. Extrusion Grade FEP is designed for processes such as tubing and film production, offering excellent flexibility and chemical resistance, ideal for medical devices. In contrast, Injection Grade FEP is tailored for mold-processing applications, providing enhanced dimensional stability and precision for components like syringes and valves. Both grades meet stringent regulatory requirements, ensuring their suitability for various medical applications and improving patient safety.</p></p>
