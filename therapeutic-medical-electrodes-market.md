<p><p>The therapeutic medical electrodes market is primarily divided into disposable and reusable types. Both types serve critical roles in various therapeutic applications, such as electrotherapy and monitoring, influencing the choice based on clinical needs, patient volume, and overall cost considerations.</p></p>
