<p><p>The ceramic insulating film market encompasses three main types of membranes. Plate type ceramic membranes feature flat surfaces ideal for filtration in water treatment and industrial processes. Tubular type ceramic membranes consist of hollow tubes, offering high surface area and efficient flow characteristics, suitable for viscous fluids. Multichannel ceramic membranes comprise multiple channels within a single unit, maximizing filtration efficiency and minimizing space requirements, making them suitable for large-scale applications in various industries such as food processing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
