<p><p>The HVAC zoning system market consists of three primary types: duct zoning systems, smart vents, and others. Duct zoning systems involve using dampers in ductwork to control airflow and temperature in different areas of a building. Smart vents are advanced devices that automatically adjust airflow based on temperature and occupancy, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency. The "others" category includes innovative solutions like wireless controls and software applications that integrate with HVAC systems for optimized zone management and monitoring.</p></p>
