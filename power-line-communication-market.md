<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) markets are categorized into narrowband and broadband segments. Narrowband PLC operates at low frequencies and is mainly used for applications like remote metering and home automation, offering low data rates for specific tasks. In contrast, broadband PLC operates at higher frequencies, providing higher data transfer rates suitable for internet access and multimedia streaming. While narrowband focuses on efficiency for specific applications, broadband caters to broader consumer needs, enabling more extensive data communication over existing power lines.</p></p>
