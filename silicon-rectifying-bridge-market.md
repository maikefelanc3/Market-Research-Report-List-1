<p><strong>The Silicon Rectifying Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-wave Rectifying Bridge</li><li>Half-wave Rectifying Bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Rectifying Bridge market is primarily divided into two types: Full-wave Rectifying Bridges and Half-wave Rectifying Bridges. Full-wave Rectifying Bridges utilize both halves of the AC cycle, converting the entire signal into DC, which leads to higher efficiency and smoother output. In contrast, Half-wave Rectifying Bridges use only one half of the AC cycle, resulting in lower efficiency and more ripple in the output. These two types cater to various applications, influencing demand based on performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
