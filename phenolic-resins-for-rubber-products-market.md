<p><p>Phenolic resins are essential in rubber products, offering durability and resistance. The market comprises two main types: thermoplastic phenolic resins, which can be melted and reshaped, providing versatility in molding applications, and thermosetting phenolic resins, which harden permanently upon curing, ensuring excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength. Thermoplastic resins are favored for applications requiring recyclability, while thermosetting resins dominate industries needing durability and heat resistance, making them crucial for high-performance rubber applications.</p></p>
