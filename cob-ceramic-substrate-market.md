<p><p>The COB ceramic substrate market is primarily divided into two types: Al2O3 COB ceramic substrates and AlN COB ceramic substrates. Al2O3, or alumina substrates, are widely used due to their excellent electrical insulation and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, AlN, or aluminum nitride substrates, offer superior thermal conductivity and a higher performance for high-power applications. Both types serve critical roles in the packaging of electronic devices, tailored to meet specific performance requirements in different industries.</p></p>
