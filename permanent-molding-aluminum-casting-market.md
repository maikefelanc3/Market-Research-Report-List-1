<p><strong>The Permanent Molding Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>Slush</li><li>Low-pressure</li><li>Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Permanent Molding Aluminum Casting market encompasses various techniques: </p><p>1. **Gravity Casting** involves pouring molten aluminum into a reusable mold using gravity, ensuring uniform thickness.</p><p>2. **Slush Casting** allows for a thin shell of aluminum to form by tilting the mold, creating hollow objects.</p><p>3. **Low-Pressure Casting** uses controlled pressure to fill the mold, enhancing material density and reducing defects.</p><p>4. **Vacuum Casting** minimizes air trapped in the mold, improving the surface finish and mechanical properties of the castings. Each method offers distinct advantages for manufacturing precision components.</p></p>
